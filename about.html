Who Are We?

At Nectar & Wick, we believe that candles should do more than just light up a room, they should inspire a sustainable lifestyle. Founded with a passion for eco-conscious living, we specialize in creating hand-poured soy candles that are as gentle on the planet as they are on your senses. Each of our candles is crafted from natural, renewable materials, from the soy wax to the sustainably sourced wicks, ensuring a clean and eco-friendly burn.

Our Brand

Our goal is not to take over the industry - just make it more transparent while helping to make cleaner choices the easy choice.

We spend a tremendous amount of time researching every ingredient we utilize in our products - both natural & synthetic.

Ethically sourced and cruelty-free being one of our top priorities. We don't have fancy packaging that just adds to already overcrowded landfills.

We never waiver from our core values and our promise to provide clean, sustainable, natural products for body and home using only the finest of ingredients and care.

Why Choose Soy?

100% Soy Wax - We utilize 100% soy, you won't find any blends sneaking in unwanted waxes here!

Phthalate Free - These can cause a myriad of health issues in certain quantities, but we omit them altogether.

Carcinogen Free - These are materials known or thought to cause cancer and are just a no-go.

Paraben Free - Irritants that we simply avoid.

Perfume Grade - We only utilize the safest, highest quality, most thoroughly tested fragrances available in our industry.
